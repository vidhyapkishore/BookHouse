    Life Is What You Make It
    Preeti Shenoy

    The story of a young girl Ankita Sharma. What would you do if the perfect life you have is torn asunder? Pretty and intelligent, Ankita has everything she wants: friends, boys in hot pursuit, and admission into a premier management school for her MBA. But six months later, she finds herself a patient in a mental hospital. How did she get here and will she ever get back her life again? A story of the pains of growing up, the strength afforded by faith and the indestructibility of the spirit, an inspiring story for modern readers.

    Burried Thoughts
    Joseph Annamkutty Jose

    Each person’s life is a grand sum of a thousand tales, told and untold, with its uphills and downhills and with its bleakness and magnificence.
